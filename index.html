What isDiscountly

Discountly is a Shopify Tiered Discount app that enables merchants to create flexible discount structures based on purchase quantities, encouraging customers to buy more and helping merchants boost their sales, AOV and customer loyalty.

With Discountly You can:

  • Boost your revenue and encourage larger orders.
  • Create flexible discount structures based on purchase quantities, making it easy to reward customers as they buy more.
  • Effortlessly set up and manage tiered discounts, offering greater value to your customers with each additional purchase.
  • Enhance customer loyalty, drive repeat sales, and watch your business grow with our user-friendly and fully customizable discount app.
